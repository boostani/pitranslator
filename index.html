Instructions

  • For ease of user, type capital L instead of λ (e.g. Lx.xy)
  • You may use prantheses if you want the expression to get process in any specific way.
  • Do not use space in your expression
  • Please make sure to type a valid lambda expression. Lambda validator is not implemented in this version.

Process

  • The translator analys the expression and disambiguate it. For example, you may enter Lx.yzx and it would update it to Lx.(yz)x.
  • After analysing the expression, the following rules will be used to translate the expression:

    M:
    [M](p)
    x
    x!p
    λx M
    [λx M](p) = p?x.p?q.[M](q)
    MN
    [M N](p) = new(a).new(b).(([M](a))|(a!b.a!p)|*((b?c).[N](c))
